Was ARPANET the internet?

Not exactly. ARPANET was an early network that contributed core architecture and practices.

What happened on January 1, 1983?

ARPANET migrated to TCP/IP in a coordinated transition known as "Flag Day."

Why did packet switching matter?

It improved resilience and efficiency by routing small packets through available paths.
